## Definition

AI veganism is a speculative and metaphorical term referring to the idea of creating artificial intelligence that learns entirely from synthetic, self-generated, or physical world data, rather than relying on human-created datasets like text, images, or code. It implies a desire for 'pure' AI that does not consume human intellectual property or labor, often discussed in the context of future autonomous agents that generate their own training environments.

### Summary

A metaphorical concept suggesting AI systems should operate without relying on human-generated data or labor.
