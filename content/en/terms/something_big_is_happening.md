## Definition

This term is not a technical definition but a cultural marker referring to periods of intense innovation, public interest, or paradigm shifts in artificial intelligence. It typically accompanies the release of transformative models, major ethical debates, or widespread adoption of AI technologies. In academic or technical contexts, it serves as a narrative device to highlight the accelerating pace of development and the societal impact of emerging AI capabilities, signaling a transition from experimental to mainstream utility.

### Summary

A colloquial phrase often used in media to describe significant breakthroughs or rapid advancements in the AI field.
