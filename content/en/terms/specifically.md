## Definition

In AI terminology, 'specifically' denotes precision in defining models, data points, or operations. It distinguishes exact parameters from general categories, ensuring clarity in technical documentation and model specifications. This term is crucial when isolating unique features or constraints that differentiate one algorithmic approach from another.

### Summary

Refers to precise, distinct, or particular details within a broader context.

- Parameter specificity
