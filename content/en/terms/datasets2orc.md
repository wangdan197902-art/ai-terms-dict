## Definition

S2ORC is a comprehensive corpus of scholarly articles derived from Semantic Scholar. It includes full-text content, metadata, and citation relationships for millions of papers across various scientific domains. This dataset is widely used for natural language processing tasks such as citation prediction, paper recommendation, and scientific information extraction. Its structured format facilitates the development of AI models that understand academic literature and research trends.

### Summary

Semantic Scholar Open Research Corpus, a large-scale dataset of academic papers with structured metadata and citation networks.
