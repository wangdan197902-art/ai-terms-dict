## Definition

AI Overviews are condensed summaries produced by large language models that aggregate and synthesize data from various web sources or databases. Unlike traditional search results that list links, these overviews provide direct answers or comprehensive explanations, enhancing user efficiency. They leverage advanced retrieval-augmented generation techniques to ensure accuracy while delivering immediate value, fundamentally changing how users consume information in digital environments.

### Summary

Summarized responses generated by AI models that synthesize information from multiple sources for quick understanding.

- Synthesis
- Summarization
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation
- Direct Answer

- Providing instant answers in search engines
- Generating executive summaries for research papers
- Offering quick explanations of complex technical concepts
