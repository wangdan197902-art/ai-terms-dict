## Definition

This concept encompasses the capacity of AI agents or systems to handle routine maintenance, resource allocation, and error correction independently. It includes features like auto-scaling, self-healing algorithms, and adaptive parameter tuning. By reducing reliance on manual oversight, self-management enhances system reliability, uptime, and efficiency in distributed cloud environments and edge computing setups.

### Summary

Self-management in AI refers to autonomous systems' ability to monitor, optimize, and repair their own operations without human intervention.

## Key Concepts

- Autonomy
- Self-healing
- Resource optimization
- Operational efficiency

## Use Cases

- Cloud infrastructure auto-scaling
- Automated model retraining pipelines
- Network traffic management
