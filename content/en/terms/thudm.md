## Definition

THUDM (Tsinghua University Natural Language Processing Research Group) is a prominent academic and research entity focused on artificial intelligence, particularly in natural language processing. They are best known for creating the ChatGLM series of large language models, which are widely used in the open-source community. Their work emphasizes efficient model architectures, bilingual capabilities, and accessibility, contributing significantly to the global AI ecosystem by releasing powerful, pre-trained models under open licenses.

### Summary

THUDM is a research group at Tsinghua University known for developing open-source AI models like ChatGLM.
