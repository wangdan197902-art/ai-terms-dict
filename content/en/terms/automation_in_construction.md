## Definition

Automation in construction refers to the integration of robotic systems, drones, and AI-driven project management tools into the building lifecycle. These technologies assist in tasks ranging from bricklaying and welding to site surveying and progress monitoring. By automating repetitive or dangerous tasks, the industry aims to enhance productivity, reduce labor shortages, and minimize workplace accidents while maintaining high quality standards.

### Summary

The application of robotics and AI to streamline building processes and improve site safety.
