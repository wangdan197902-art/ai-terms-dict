## Definition

Grok is a large language model chatbot created by Elon Musk's company, xAI. It is primarily accessible to subscribers of the X platform (formerly Twitter). Grok distinguishes itself by having real-time access to the vast amount of data posted on X, allowing it to provide up-to-date information and context-aware responses. It features a 'Fun Mode' that enables it to respond with a rebellious, sarcastic, and witty tone, mimicking the style of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.

### Summary

An AI chatbot developed by xAI, integrated into the X platform, known for its real-time access to social media data and witty personality.
