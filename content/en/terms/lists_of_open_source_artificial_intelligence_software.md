## Definition

These refer to organized repositories, such as GitHub topics, Awesome lists, or community-maintained wikis, that aggregate open-source software related to artificial intelligence. They serve as essential resources for developers and researchers to discover tools for machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and reinforcement learning. Examples include 'Awesome AI' or specific framework indexes. These lists facilitate knowledge sharing, reduce duplication of effort, and help practitioners identify robust, community-supported solutions for various AI tasks.

### Summary

Curated collections or directories cataloging publicly available AI tools, libraries, and frameworks.
