## Definition

This field studies the processes behind how ideas are formed, combined, and evolved. It applies structured techniques to enhance creativity and problem-solving capabilities. In AI contexts, ideonomy can refer to algorithms designed to generate novel hypotheses or solutions by exploring vast conceptual spaces systematically rather than randomly.

### Summary

Ideonomy is the science of idea generation, focusing on systematic methods for creating and organizing new concepts and innovations.
