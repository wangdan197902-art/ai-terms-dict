## Definition

Created by the University of Helsinki and Reaktor, this educational initiative aims to demystify AI for the general public. It covers fundamental topics such as machine learning, deep learning, ethics, and the future of work. The course emphasizes understanding what AI can and cannot do, helping learners navigate the technological landscape with informed perspectives rather than technical implementation details.

### Summary

Elements of AI is a free online course designed to provide a broad, non-technical introduction to artificial intelligence concepts and their societal impact.
