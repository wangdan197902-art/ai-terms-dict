## Definition

Operation Serenata de Amor is a pioneering open-source project that applies artificial intelligence to analyze public procurement data in Brazil. By utilizing natural language processing and anomaly detection algorithms, it identifies potential irregularities in government contracts, thereby promoting transparency and accountability. This initiative demonstrates how AI can serve democratic processes by empowering citizens and journalists to uncover corruption through data-driven insights.

### Summary

A civic tech initiative using machine learning to detect fraud in Brazilian public spending.
