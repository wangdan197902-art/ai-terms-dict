## Definition

Kimi K2 represents a significant iteration in Moonshot AI's series of large language models. It is characterized by its enhanced capabilities in complex logical reasoning, mathematical problem-solving, and natural language processing. The model supports extremely long context windows, allowing it to process and analyze vast amounts of information simultaneously. It is optimized for both Chinese and English tasks, aiming to provide high-quality assistance in professional and creative domains through improved alignment and efficiency.

### Summary

Kimi K2 is a large language model developed by Moonshot AI, designed for advanced reasoning and long-context understanding.
