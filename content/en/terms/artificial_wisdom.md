## Definition

Artificial wisdom (AW) is an emerging concept that seeks to augment artificial intelligence with human-like values, ethical considerations, and long-term strategic judgment. While AI focuses on efficiency and pattern recognition, AW aims to incorporate moral reasoning, cultural context, and holistic understanding into decision-making processes. It addresses the limitations of pure data-driven approaches by integrating normative frameworks, ensuring that automated systems act in ways that are not only effective but also socially responsible and aligned with human well-being.

### Summary

The extension of AI beyond intelligence to include judgment, ethics, and contextual understanding for sound decision-making.
