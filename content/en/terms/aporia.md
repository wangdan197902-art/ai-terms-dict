## Definition

In philosophy and AI theory, aporia describes a paradoxical situation where two equally valid arguments lead to contradictory outcomes. In machine learning, this might manifest when a model's performance metrics conflict with its ethical implications or when interpretability methods yield inconsistent explanations. Recognizing aporia helps researchers identify fundamental limitations in current frameworks, prompting deeper investigation into model behavior and theoretical consistency rather than accepting superficial solutions.
