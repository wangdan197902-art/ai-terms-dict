## Definition

Harmful content refers to digital media or text that can cause physical, psychological, or social damage. In AI safety, detecting and filtering such content is critical to prevent models from generating toxic outputs. This includes categories like misinformation, harassment, self-harm promotion, and extremist propaganda. Robust moderation systems utilize natural language processing to identify patterns associated with these dangers, ensuring platforms remain safe and compliant with ethical guidelines and legal standards.

### Summary

Information that poses risks to individuals or society, including hate speech, violence, and illegal acts.
