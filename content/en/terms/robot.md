## Definition

A robot is an autonomous or semi-autonomous mechanical device designed to perform tasks either independently or under remote control. It typically consists of sensors for environmental perception, actuators for physical movement or manipulation, and a processing unit running algorithms to make decisions. Modern robots integrate artificial intelligence to adapt to changing conditions, enabling applications ranging from industrial manufacturing to surgical precision and domestic assistance.

### Summary

A programmable machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically.
