## Definition

Time series data consists of observations recorded sequentially over time intervals. In AI, this data type is crucial for predicting future trends based on historical patterns. Specialized models like ARIMA, LSTM, and Transformer-based architectures are employed to capture temporal dependencies, seasonality, and trends. Accurate time series analysis enables applications ranging from stock market prediction to energy consumption forecasting and sensor data monitoring.

### Summary

A sequence of data points indexed in time order, commonly analyzed using specialized AI models for forecasting.
