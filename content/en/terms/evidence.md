## Definition

In artificial intelligence, evidence refers to empirical data, statistical results, or observable outcomes that substantiate claims about model behavior, accuracy, or effectiveness. It serves as the foundation for decision-making processes, allowing researchers and engineers to verify whether a machine learning algorithm has learned the intended patterns from its training data. Without robust evidence, AI systems lack credibility and reliability in practical applications.

### Summary

Data or information used to support a hypothesis or validate an AI model's performance.
