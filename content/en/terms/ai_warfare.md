## Definition

AI warfare refers to the integration of artificial intelligence into military strategies, including autonomous drones, predictive logistics, cyber defense, and decision-support systems for commanders. It encompasses both defensive applications, such as threat detection, and offensive capabilities, like lethal autonomous weapons systems (LAWS). This field raises significant ethical and legal questions regarding accountability, escalation risks, and the potential for algorithmic bias in combat scenarios.

### Summary

The application of artificial intelligence technologies to military operations, surveillance, and autonomous weapons systems.

## Key Concepts

- Lethal Autonomous Weapons
- Military Strategy
- Cyber Warfare
- Algorithmic Accountability

## Use Cases

- Drone swarm coordination
- Predictive maintenance for military hardware
- Intelligence analysis and threat detection
