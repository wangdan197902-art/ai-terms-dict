## Definition

The HF ASR Leaderboard is a community-driven metric platform hosted by Hugging Face, tracking state-of-the-art performance in Automatic Speech Recognition. It allows researchers and developers to benchmark models against standard datasets like Common Voice or LibriSpeech. By providing transparent evaluation metrics such as Word Error Rate (WER), it facilitates progress tracking and encourages the sharing of high-quality pre-trained models within the open-source AI ecosystem.

### Summary

A ranking system on Hugging Face that evaluates and compares the performance of Automatic Speech Recognition models.
