## Definition

Supermind AI refers to systems where multiple AI components, human experts, or hybrid human-AI teams collaborate seamlessly to form a collective intelligence that exceeds the capability of any individual part. This approach leverages diverse perspectives and specialized modules to tackle complex tasks. It emphasizes synergy, where the whole is greater than the sum of its parts, often used in decision support systems, creative collaboration tools, and complex scientific discovery processes.

### Summary

An AI architecture or system designed to integrate multiple intelligent agents or models into a unified, enhanced cognitive entity capable of superior problem-solving.

## Key Concepts

- Collective Intelligence
- Human-AI Collaboration
- Modular Architecture
- Synergy

## Use Cases

- Complex scientific research assistance
- Multi-agent decision support systems
- Creative co-pilots for design and writing
