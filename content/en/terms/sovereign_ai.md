## Definition

Sovereign AI describes the capability of a country or organization to build, deploy, and manage artificial intelligence systems independently, without reliance on foreign cloud providers or proprietary models. This concept emphasizes data residency, local compute resources, and customized models trained on national datasets. It aims to protect sensitive information from external surveillance or geopolitical leverage while fostering domestic innovation. By retaining full control over the AI lifecycle, entities can align technological development with local laws, cultural values, and security requirements.

### Summary

A strategic approach where nations or entities develop and control their own AI infrastructure and models to ensure data privacy and regulatory independence.

## Key Concepts

- Data Residency
- Local Compute
- Regulatory Autonomy
- National Security

## Use Cases

- Government cloud infrastructure
- Cross-border data protection
- Domestic tech industry growth
