## Definition

Machine learning enhances earth sciences by processing satellite imagery, seismic data, and climate records to model complex environmental systems. These techniques help predict weather patterns, monitor deforestation, and assess earthquake risks with greater accuracy. By identifying subtle correlations in large datasets, ML supports sustainable resource management and disaster preparedness, offering critical insights into planetary changes over time.

### Summary

The use of machine learning algorithms to analyze geospatial and environmental data for predicting natural phenomena and managing resources.

## Key Concepts

- Geospatial Analysis
- Climate Modeling
- Remote Sensing
- Environmental Monitoring

## Use Cases

- Predicting hurricane paths and intensity
- Monitoring glacier melt rates via satellite imagery
- Detecting illegal logging activities in forests
