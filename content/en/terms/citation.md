## Definition

As generative AI models produce content, the need for citation mechanisms has emerged to maintain academic integrity and legal compliance. This involves embedding references to original sources within AI-generated outputs, allowing users to verify claims and trace information back to its origin. Advanced systems are being developed to automatically generate bibliographies or highlight quoted segments, addressing issues of hallucination and copyright infringement in knowledge-intensive applications like research assistants.

### Summary

Citation in AI refers to the practice of attributing source material or data used within generated text or models to ensure transparency and intellectual property compliance.
