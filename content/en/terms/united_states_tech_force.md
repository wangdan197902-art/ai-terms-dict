## Definition

The term 'United States Tech Force' generally denotes the large segment of the American labor market employed in technology sectors, including software engineering, data science, hardware manufacturing, and IT services. This workforce is critical to the nation's economic competitiveness and innovation capacity. Discussions around this term often involve topics such as workforce shortages, immigration policies affecting tech talent, educational pipelines, and the impact of automation on traditional tech roles. It represents a key asset in the global race for technological supremacy.

### Summary

A demographic and economic term referring to the collective workforce driving technological innovation in the US.
