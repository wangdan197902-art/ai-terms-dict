## Definition

In AI ethics, consent refers to the voluntary and informed permission granted by users or subjects before their personal data is collected, stored, or utilized in machine learning models. It requires transparency regarding how data will be used, potential risks, and the right to withdraw permission. Valid consent is a cornerstone of privacy regulations like GDPR, ensuring that individuals maintain agency over their digital footprint and protecting them from non-consensual surveillance or exploitation by AI systems.

### Summary

The explicit, informed agreement by individuals to allow their data to be collected and processed for AI purposes.
