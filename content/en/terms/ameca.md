## Definition

Ameca is a state-of-the-art humanoid robot featuring over 40 degrees of freedom in its face alone, allowing for subtle and realistic emotional expressions. Designed to study human-robot interaction, it can mimic complex social cues and engage in natural conversations. Its development focuses on bridging the gap between mechanical movement and genuine human empathy, making it a significant milestone in robotics and affective computing research.

### Summary

A highly advanced humanoid robot developed by Engineered Arts, known for its exceptional facial expressiveness and human-like interactions.
