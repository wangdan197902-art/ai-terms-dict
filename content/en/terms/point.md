## Definition

A point in AI contexts usually denotes a discrete coordinate within a feature space or embedding vector. For instance, in clustering algorithms like K-Means, each data sample is treated as a point in N-dimensional space. Understanding the geometric relationships between points, such as distance and similarity, is fundamental for tasks like classification, retrieval, and dimensionality reduction.

### Summary

In AI mathematics, a point represents a specific location in a multi-dimensional vector space, often used in embeddings or coordinate systems.
