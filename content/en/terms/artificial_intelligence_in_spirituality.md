## Definition

AI in spirituality refers to the application of artificial intelligence in religious or contemplative contexts. This includes chatbots offering moral advice, AI-generated art for meditation, or algorithms analyzing sacred texts. Some view AI as a tool for enhancing mindfulness, while others debate whether machines can possess consciousness or soul-like qualities. This field raises profound questions about the nature of consciousness, the role of technology in faith, and the boundaries between human spirituality and machine simulation.

### Summary

The emerging intersection where AI is used for spiritual guidance, meditation assistance, or exploring philosophical questions.
