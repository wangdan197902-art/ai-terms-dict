## Definition

Vibevoice is a conceptual or branded approach to Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology that emphasizes capturing the 'vibe' or emotional nuance of human speech. Unlike traditional TTS which may sound monotone, vibevoice models integrate prosody, intonation, and subtle emotional cues to create more engaging and lifelike audio outputs. This is often achieved through advanced transformer architectures trained on diverse, emotionally labeled datasets, making it suitable for interactive companions and immersive media.

### Summary

Vibevoice refers to a style of AI-generated speech synthesis that prioritizes natural, emotive, and contextually aware vocal delivery over rigid robotic precision.
