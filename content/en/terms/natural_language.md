## Definition

Natural language refers to the way humans speak and write, including all its ambiguities, idioms, and cultural nuances. In AI, processing natural language involves understanding syntax, semantics, and pragmatics to enable machines to interpret and generate human-like text. This field underpins technologies like chatbots, translation services, and sentiment analysis, bridging the gap between human intent and machine execution.

### Summary

The standard human communication method, characterized by grammar, vocabulary, and context, as opposed to formal computer languages.
