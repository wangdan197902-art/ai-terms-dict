## Definition

The Genesis Mission typically refers to a strategic phase or project within an organization aimed at laying the groundwork for advanced AI capabilities. This involves setting up core infrastructure, defining ethical boundaries, selecting initial models, and establishing governance protocols. It serves as the starting point for integrating generative AI into business workflows, ensuring that subsequent developments are aligned with corporate values, regulatory requirements, and technical standards before scaling across departments.

### Summary

An organizational initiative focused on establishing foundational AI principles, infrastructure, and ethical guidelines for future generative AI development.

- Strategic Foundation
- Ethical Frameworks
- Infrastructure Setup
- Pilot Programs

- Corporate AI strategy formulation
- Initial model selection and testing
- Establishing AI ethics committees
