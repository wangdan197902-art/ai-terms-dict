## Definition

While not a technical AI algorithmic term, 'instead' is crucial in prompt engineering and natural language understanding. It signals a contrast or substitution relationship between clauses. In LLM training, recognizing such discourse markers helps models understand intent, follow negative constraints, and generate responses that offer alternatives rather than executing the primary requested action.

### Summary

Instead is a linguistic conjunction or adverb indicating substitution, replacement, or an alternative action taken in place of another.
