## Definition

GooAQ is a dataset compiled from the Google Answers service, featuring a massive collection of user-submitted questions along with detailed, paid responses. It serves as a valuable resource for training models in open-domain question answering and information retrieval. The diversity of topics and the structured nature of the Q&A pairs allow researchers to develop systems capable of understanding complex user intents and retrieving relevant factual information from vast corpora.

### Summary

A large-scale dataset containing Google Answers queries and responses, used for training information retrieval and question answering models.
