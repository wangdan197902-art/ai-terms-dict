## Definition

In the context of general language processing, 'haw' is an informal exclamation or hesitation sound. While it does not represent a core algorithmic concept in artificial intelligence, NLP models must understand its pragmatic function in human conversation. It may indicate uncertainty, disagreement, or serve as a filler in spoken dialogue transcripts. Recognizing such non-lexical utterances helps improve speech-to-text accuracy and sentiment analysis in conversational AI systems.

### Summary

A colloquial interjection often used in informal digital communication, lacking specific technical meaning in AI contexts.
