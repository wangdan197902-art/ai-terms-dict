## Definition

In artificial intelligence, an autonomous agent is an entity that operates independently within an environment. It uses sensors to perceive states and actuators to perform actions, guided by an internal decision-making process or policy. These agents can adapt to dynamic changes and pursue objectives over time, ranging from simple reflex-based bots to complex systems like self-driving cars or robotic explorers. Their autonomy level varies based on the degree of human oversight required during operation.

### Summary

An autonomous agent is a system capable of perceiving its environment and acting independently to achieve specific goals without direct human intervention.
