## Definition

The Artificial Inventor Project is an interdisciplinary research effort aimed at understanding and replicating the cognitive mechanisms behind human creativity and invention. It seeks to build AI systems capable of generating novel ideas, solving ill-defined problems, and mimicking the intuitive leaps often seen in human inventors. By studying how humans combine disparate concepts to form new solutions, this project contributes to the broader field of computational creativity, aiming to create tools that assist rather than replace human innovators in design and engineering tasks.

### Summary

A research initiative focused on developing computational models of human-like creative problem-solving and invention processes.
