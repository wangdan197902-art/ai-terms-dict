## Definition

DeepSeek refers to a family of artificial intelligence models created by the company DeepSeek. These models are designed to handle complex natural language processing tasks, including code generation, logical reasoning, and multilingual understanding. DeepSeek has gained prominence in the AI community for releasing powerful open-weight models that compete with proprietary counterparts while maintaining high computational efficiency. Their architecture often employs advanced techniques like Mixture of Experts (MoE) to optimize inference speed and resource utilization without sacrificing performance on benchmark tests.

### Summary

DeepSeek is a series of large language models developed by DeepSeek, known for their high efficiency, strong coding capabilities, and open-weight availability.
