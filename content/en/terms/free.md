## Definition

In the context of AI, 'free' typically refers to open-source models, datasets, or tools that users can access, modify, and distribute without paying fees. This contrasts with proprietary solutions that require subscriptions or licenses. Free resources accelerate research and development by allowing the community to build upon existing work, though they may lack the support or performance guarantees of commercial alternatives. It emphasizes accessibility and collaborative innovation within the AI ecosystem.

### Summary

Describes resources, software, or data that are available without financial cost or restrictive licensing.
