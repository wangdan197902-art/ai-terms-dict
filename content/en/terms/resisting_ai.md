## Definition

Resisting AI refers to methods used by individuals or entities to avoid being influenced, tracked, or classified by AI algorithms. This includes adversarial attacks on perception systems, privacy-preserving data obfuscation, or behavioral changes designed to break predictive models. While often associated with malicious evasion, it also encompasses legitimate privacy advocacy and robustness testing against algorithmic bias or surveillance.

### Summary

Strategies and techniques employed to evade detection, manipulation, or control by artificial intelligence systems.
