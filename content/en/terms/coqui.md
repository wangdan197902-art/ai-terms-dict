## Definition

Coqui Technologies was a prominent player in the open-source AI community, best known for its TTS (Text-to-Speech) engine. The project provided pre-trained models capable of generating natural-sounding speech in multiple languages with minimal data requirements. Although the company ceased operations, its codebase and models remain widely used in the developer community for applications requiring voice synthesis, serving as a foundational tool for many speech-related AI projects.

### Summary

Coqui is an open-source speech technology company known for developing high-quality, multilingual text-to-speech models.
