## Definition

AI in education involves using machine learning, natural language processing, and adaptive systems to improve educational outcomes. It enables personalized learning paths tailored to individual student needs, automated grading, and intelligent tutoring systems. Educators use AI to identify at-risk students and optimize curriculum design. While it offers efficiency and customization, it also raises questions about data privacy, the role of teachers, and equitable access to technology in diverse educational settings.

### Summary

The application of AI technologies to enhance teaching methods, personalize learning experiences, and automate administrative tasks.

## Key Concepts

- Personalized learning
- Adaptive systems
- Automated assessment
- Intelligent tutoring

## Use Cases

- Student performance prediction
- Automated essay grading
- Curriculum recommendation engines
