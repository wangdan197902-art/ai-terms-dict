## Definition

In the context of AI and technology, 'CSM' is not a universally standardized term like 'CNN' or 'RNN'. It most commonly stands for Contextual Speech Models in speech processing research, referring to systems that incorporate broader contextual information to improve transcription accuracy. Alternatively, in enterprise IT, it may refer to Cloud Security Management. Without additional context, the term remains ambiguous, and its precise meaning depends heavily on the specific industry or sub-field being discussed.

### Summary

An ambiguous acronym that typically refers to Contextual Speech Models or Cloud Security Management in specific technical domains, lacking a universal standard definition in general AI.
