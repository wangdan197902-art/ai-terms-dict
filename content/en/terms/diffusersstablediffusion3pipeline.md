## Definition

This pipeline utilizes the Stable Diffusion 3 model, which introduces a Multimodal Diffusion Transformer (MMDiT) architecture and Flow Matching training objective. These advancements significantly enhance the model's ability to render legible text within images, improve compositional accuracy, and reduce artifacts compared to previous diffusion models. It offers higher fidelity and better prompt adherence for complex scenes.

### Summary

A pipeline implementing Stable Diffusion 3's architecture, featuring MMDiT and Flow Matching for improved image quality and text rendering.

## Key Concepts

- MMDiT Architecture
- Flow Matching
- Text Rendering
- High-Fidelity Generation

## Use Cases

- Generating images with accurate, readable text overlays
- Creating complex compositions with multiple subjects
- Producing high-resolution artwork with minimal artifacts
