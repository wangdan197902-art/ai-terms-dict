## Definition

In mathematics and theoretical computer science, a group is a set G together with a binary operation that satisfies four axioms: closure, associativity, identity, and invertibility. In AI, group theory is often applied to handle symmetries in data.

### Summary

En fundamental matematisk struktur bestående af en mængde udstyret med en operation, der kombinerer ethvert to elementer til et tredje element.
